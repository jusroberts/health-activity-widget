Privacy Policy

Health Activity Widget — last updated March 2026

Overview

Health Activity Widget is a home screen widget that displays your physical activity as a contribution-style grid. We take your privacy seriously: your health data never leaves your device.

Data we access

With your explicit permission, the app reads the following data from Android Health Connect on your device:

  • Step count records
  • Exercise session records (type and date only)

How we use your data

Health data is read locally on your device solely to render the activity grid in the widget. It is:

  • Never transmitted to any server, cloud service, or third party.
  • Never stored beyond what Android Health Connect itself retains.
  • Never shared with advertisers, analytics providers, or any other service.

The only preference data the app stores locally is your widget display settings (chosen colors, which activity types to show). This data remains on your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

Permissions

The app requests the following Health Connect permissions:

  • READ_STEPS — to count daily steps toward your goal
  • READ_EXERCISE — to identify exercise session dates and types

You can revoke these permissions at any time through the Health Connect app or Android Settings. Revoking permissions will cause the widget to display an empty grid until access is re-granted.

Children's privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes materially, the updated version will be published at this URL with a revised date at the top.

Contact
