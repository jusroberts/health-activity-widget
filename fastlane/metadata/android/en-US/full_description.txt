Health Activity Widget shows your Android Health Connect data as a GitHub-style contribution calendar — a 13-week grid where each cell represents one day, lighting up when an activity is present.

FEATURES

• Step goal tracking — cells highlight when you reach 10,000 steps
• Exercise session tracking — any exercise type recorded in Health Connect
• Multi-colour bands — days with multiple activity types show each colour
• Per-activity colour picker — choose a colour for each activity type
• Per-activity toggles — hide activity types you don't care about
• Hourly background refresh plus a manual refresh button if you can't wait
• Resizable widget (but I suggest the default 2x1 or 3x2)

PRIVACY

All data is read directly from Health Connect on your device. Nothing is transmitted anywhere. No internet permission is requested. No analytics, no ads, no accounts.

REQUIREMENTS

Android 8.0 (API 26) or higher and the Health Connect app installed with activity data recorded.

Note: Health Connect is distributed by Google. On de-Googled ROMs where Health Connect is not available, the widget cannot display data.
